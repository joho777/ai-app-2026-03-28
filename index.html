About DotClaude Lab
A hands-on model for how agent workspaces become shareable, reproducible, and safe to keep.
What is this?
Many AI agents persist state across runs (plans, tool outputs, summaries, configs). Some systems store those artifacts in a hidden folder. This app lets you explore a synthetic .claude/ structure, simulate runs, and build a shareable bundle.
Try these experiments
  • Switch presets and see how the number/type of artifacts changes.
  • Enable Redact privacy and regenerate the bundle.
  • Pick Keep within size budget and apply retention.
  • Create a new file with a fake secret and watch the risk score increase.
  • Share a snapshot link and open it in another tab to reproduce the workspace.
How risk is estimated
This demo computes a "leakage risk" score from heuristics: how many logs you keep, whether you store prompts/tool outputs, and whether content looks like credentials or personal data.
No network, no uploads
Everything runs locally in your browser. Exported bundles are generated on-device.
